# Retail Sales Performance Dashboard

This repository contains a comprehensive **Retail Sales Performance Dashboard** built using **Power BI**. It provides detailed insights into retail sales data, allowing businesses to track key performance metrics, product sales distribution, and identify trends.

## Features
- **KPI Metrics**: Total Sales, Average Order Value, Sales Growth.
- **Sales by Product Category**: Visualizes sales data for **Beauty**, **Electronics**, and **Clothing**.
- **Sales Trend Over Time**: A line chart to display sales trends over months and years.
- **Sales Distribution by Product Category**: A pie chart to show the contribution of each product category to total sales.
- **Interactive Filters**: Includes **Date** and **Product Category** slicers to allow users to filter data dynamically.
- **Sales Ranking**: Visualizes the best-selling products and categories.

## Recommendations for Business

1. **Target Marketing for High-Sales Demographics**:
   - Focus on the **female demographic** and **age group 40 to 49**, as these groups are the most engaged.
   - Optimize **Clothing** and **Beauty** categories to capture more of the **female consumer** base.

2. **Investigate Bulk Purchases for B2B Opportunities**:
   - Explore the **outliers in total amount** for possible **bulk buyers** and offer **wholesale** deals or discounts.

3. **Boost Sales in Slow-Selling Product Categories**:
   - **Beauty** products are underperforming. Introduce **new lines** or **promotions** in this category to improve its sales share.

4. **Improve Sales During Low-Selling Months**:
   - Focus on **January** and other low months by offering **discounts**, running **targeted campaigns**, or launching new products to sustain sales throughout the year.

5. **Increase Average Order Value (AOV)**:
   - Consider **bundling** products, offering **loyalty rewards**, or **upselling** during checkout to increase the average order value.
